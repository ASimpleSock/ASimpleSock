MASHAAL

Tool for Self Assessment (Influencing)


Here’s a simple self-assessment that can be used to get a snapshot of your ability and skills in the influencing area.

Name
Rate yourself in terms of whether you do these things:
1. When things get emotional, I have to disengage

2. When people are forceful with me, I agree with things I usually wouldn’t.

3. When dealing with people, I get frustrated when they don’t listen to and heed my advice.

4. If I avoid uncomfortable situations, others will work it out on their own.

5. I don’t understand why people have to get emotional. I wonder why they just don’t do their job.

6. I don’t get paid to be sensitive to people’s feelings.

7. I usually lose in negotiations. I tend to give up on what I really want.

8. I respond to people ‘like for like’. If they get angry with me, I get angry back.

9. I’m not paid to be a mediator; I’m paid to get projects done.

10. When people do things for me (whether or not I have asked for it), I feel obligated to reciprocate.
